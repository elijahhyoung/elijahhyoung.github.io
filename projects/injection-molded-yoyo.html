Challenge

Translate a product concept into an injection-molded object where geometry, tooling, shrinkage, finish, and assembly all affect the final physical quality.

Constraints

  • Geometry had to support injection molding and post-mold assembly.
  • Tooling decisions had to account for manufacturability and product finish.
  • Process planning had to connect design intent to a repeatable physical result.
