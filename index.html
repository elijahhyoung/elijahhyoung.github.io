About

I'd rather be in the shop than in a simulation.

I'm an MIT mechanical engineering student, and my favorite projects are the ones where you can't fake it: you design something, build it, watch it fail in a new way, and go again. That loop is more or less why I keep signing up for hands-on classes and lab work instead of easier electives.

Working Notes

  • Test early, even when it's ugly.
  • Find the real constraint before falling in love with a design.
  • A rough prototype beats a perfect simulation.
