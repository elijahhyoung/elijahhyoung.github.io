About

I like engineering work where the model, the shop, and the test stand all get a vote.

I am an MIT mechanical engineering student drawn to physical systems that are difficult to make work outside a simulation. The work that interests me most has tight feedback loops: design the mechanism, fabricate it, measure what changed, and use the evidence to make the next version better.
